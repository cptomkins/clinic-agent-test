# Billing FAQ

## When am I charged?

ClinicSoft bills on the same date each month (or year, for annual plans). Your billing date is the day you first subscribed. You can see your next billing date in **Settings > Billing & Plan**.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. You can update your card in **Settings > Billing & Plan > Payment Method**. We do not currently support ACH, wire transfers, or invoicing for monthly plans. Annual plans for 5+ seats can be paid by invoice — contact support to set this up.

## Why was I charged a different amount?

The most common reasons are a mid-cycle seat change (prorated charges), a plan upgrade (prorated difference), or taxes applied based on your billing address. Check **Settings > Billing & Plan > Invoice History** for a line-by-line breakdown of each charge.

## Can I get a refund?

We offer a full refund within 14 days of any charge if you contact support. After 14 days, charges are non-refundable, but you can downgrade or cancel to prevent future charges. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

## How do seat charges work?

Each seat is a user who can log in to ClinicSoft. You're charged per seat on your plan's per-seat rate. Providers, front desk staff, and office managers each need their own seat. Adding a seat mid-cycle is prorated. Removing a seat takes effect at the next billing date.

## Annual vs monthly billing

Annual billing saves roughly 15% compared to monthly. You can switch from monthly to annual at any time in **Settings > Billing & Plan**. The annual rate is charged immediately for the full year, minus a credit for any unused time on your current monthly cycle. Switching from annual to monthly takes effect at the end of your annual term.

## Tax and invoices

ClinicSoft charges applicable sales tax based on your billing address. All invoices are available in **Settings > Billing & Plan > Invoice History** as downloadable PDFs. If you need a W-9 or tax exemption applied, contact support.
