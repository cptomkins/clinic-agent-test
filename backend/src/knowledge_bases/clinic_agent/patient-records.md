# Patient Records

## Finding a Patient

Click **Patients** in the left sidebar to open the patient list. Use the search bar at the top to find a patient by name, date of birth, phone number, or email. Results update as you type. Click a patient row to open their full record.

## Patient Profile

The patient profile shows contact information (phone, email, address), date of birth, insurance details, and emergency contact at the top. Below that are tabs for **Appointments**, **Billing**, **Documents**, and **Notes**.

## Adding a New Patient

Click **+ New Patient** from the patient list. Required fields are first name, last name, and date of birth. Phone, email, address, and insurance information are optional but recommended. After saving, the patient is immediately available for appointment booking.

## Editing Contact Information

Open the patient profile and click the **Edit** icon next to the contact section. Update phone, email, or address fields and click **Save**. Changes take effect immediately and are reflected everywhere the patient's contact info appears.

## Merging Duplicate Records

If a patient was accidentally created twice, go to **Settings > Patient Management > Merge Records**. Search for both records, select them, and click **Preview Merge**. ClinicSoft shows which fields will be kept from each record. Confirm to merge — all appointments, billing records, and notes from both records are combined under a single patient ID.

## Insurance Information

In the patient profile, click the **Insurance** tab. Enter the carrier name, plan ID, group number, and subscriber information. ClinicSoft validates the format but does not verify coverage in real time. Insurance details are automatically pulled into new claims filed for this patient.
