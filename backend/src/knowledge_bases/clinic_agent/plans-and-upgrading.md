# Plans & Upgrading

## Plan Comparison

ClinicSoft offers three plans:

**Free** — For solo practitioners getting started. Includes 1 provider, 1 user seat, basic scheduling, and patient records. Limited to 50 active patients. No insurance claim filing, no reporting, no email reminders.

**Standard ($49/month per seat)** — For small to mid-size practices. Includes unlimited providers, unlimited patients, insurance claim filing, automated appointment reminders (email and SMS), basic reporting, and payment tracking. Supports up to 10 user seats.

**Pro ($89/month per seat)** — Everything in Standard, plus advanced reporting and analytics, waitlist management, recurring appointment series, custom form builder, multi-location support, API access, and priority support with a dedicated account manager. Unlimited user seats.

All plans include a 14-day free trial of Pro features for new accounts.

## How to Upgrade

Go to **Settings > Billing & Plan**. Your current plan is shown at the top. Click **Change Plan** to see available options. Select the new plan and confirm. Upgrades take effect immediately — you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing cycle.

## Adding or Removing Seats

In **Settings > Billing & Plan**, click **Manage Seats**. Increase or decrease the number of user seats. Adding a seat is prorated to your current billing cycle. Removing a seat takes effect at the next billing date — the user can continue accessing ClinicSoft until then. You cannot remove a seat if it would drop below the number of active users; deactivate a user first.

## Downgrading

You can downgrade at any time from **Settings > Billing & Plan**. The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. If you're moving to a plan with fewer features, ClinicSoft will warn you about which features you'll lose access to. Your data is never deleted on downgrade — if you upgrade again later, everything is still there.

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription, go to **Settings > Billing & Plan > Cancel Subscription**. Your account stays active until the end of the current billing period. After that, you're moved to the Free plan with its limitations. Data is retained for 12 months after cancellation.
