# Scheduling

## Viewing the Schedule

Open **Schedule** from the left sidebar. The default view shows today's appointments for all providers. Use the date picker at the top to navigate to a different day, or toggle between Day, Week, and Month views. Click any provider's name in the filter bar to show only their appointments.

## Booking an Appointment

Click an empty time slot on the schedule grid, or use the **+ New Appointment** button in the top-right corner. Select a patient from the dropdown (start typing to search by name), choose a provider, pick an appointment type, and set the duration. ClinicSoft will warn you if the slot conflicts with an existing appointment for that provider. Click **Save** to confirm.

## Rescheduling

Click an existing appointment on the schedule to open its detail panel. Click **Reschedule**, then pick a new date and time. ClinicSoft checks for conflicts at the new time before confirming. The patient's appointment history will show both the original and rescheduled times.

## Cancelling

Open the appointment detail panel and click **Cancel Appointment**. You'll be asked to select a reason (patient request, no-show, provider unavailable, other). Cancelled appointments remain visible in the patient's history with a strikethrough, but are removed from the active schedule view.

## Recurring Appointments

To set up a recurring series (e.g. biweekly cleanings), book the first appointment, then check **Repeat** in the detail panel. Choose a frequency (weekly, biweekly, monthly) and an end date or number of occurrences. Each occurrence is created as a separate appointment that can be individually rescheduled or cancelled.

## Waitlist

If a patient wants an earlier slot, click **Add to Waitlist** from their appointment detail panel. When a cancellation opens a matching slot, ClinicSoft shows a notification in the sidebar with a one-click option to move the waitlisted patient into the open slot.
